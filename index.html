Reading

I also love Percy Jackson & the Olympians because it brings myths to life in such a fun, easy way. And honestly, I’m hooked on pretty much all of Brandon Mull’s books. His stories are full of magic and epic adventures that keep me coming back for more. For me, reading isn’t just about passing time — it’s about escaping into different worlds and getting inspired.

History
